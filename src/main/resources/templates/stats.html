Learning Analytics

Here you can find a range of analytics which can help you prepare your class or to improve your story. You can of course download all data as CSV using the button below.

How much time does each decision take?

If your students spend a long time before making a certain decision, this could point to several things:

  • Both options are equally good
  • Both options are equally bad
  • This decision might be an ethical dilemma

Analyse the decisions that take the longest and see if you can find interesting points to discuss in class!

Where do people end the story?

Seeing where people end the story can be interesting to monitor completion rate. If many students abort the story at the same point, this might point to confusing narration, a morally difficult decision, or a boring story. Discuss in class what possible reasons are!
